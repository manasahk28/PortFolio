About Me

Hi! I'm Manasa H K, a 3rd-year B.Tech (Hons) Computer Science and Engineering student at RV University, Bengaluru, specialising in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML). I'm passionate about building intelligent, efficient, and user-centered technology that solves real-world problems.

I enjoy working at the intersection of AI, full-stack development, and cloud technologies. I am currently building projects such as a Self-Correcting Multi-Agent NLP Workflow and a Misinformation Intent Detection system, focusing on intelligent, research-driven model development. Alongside these, I'm refining full-stack applications like the Online Auction System and the ATM Interface Simulation. These ongoing projects continue to strengthen my backend engineering skills, deepen my understanding of modern AI workflows, and enhance my ability to design clean, user-friendly interfaces.

I love collaborating, exploring new tools, and taking on challenges that push me to learn more. My current interests include AIML, deep learning, full-stack development, cloud deployment, and building end-to-end scalable applications.

Outside academics and coding, I enjoy sketching, designing, photography, and taking up courses that fuel my creativity and technical growth. I'm actively seeking internships, research roles, and impactful tech projects where I can apply my skills and continue learning.

I'm excited to build meaningful solutions and contribute to innovative projects - one purposeful line of code at a time. 🚀
